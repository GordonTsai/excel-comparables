Interactive Brokers Group, Inc. operates as an automated electronic broker and market maker in the United States and internationally. The company is involved in routing orders; and executing and processing trades in securities, futures, foreign exchange instruments, bonds, and mutual funds on approximately 100 electronic exchanges and trading venues. It also provides bid and offer quotations on approximately one million securities and futures products listed on electronic exchanges. The company serves institutional and individual customers, as well as services approximately 281,000 cleared customer accounts. Interactive Brokers Group, Inc. was founded in 1977 and is headquartered in Greenwich, Connecticut.
